MR. HOANG LE
Leader of team Mobile
Engma Engineering Services Trading Joint Stock company

1.What kind of work is done by the IT professional daily?

About the IT job is very broad. I only speak on a few software developments. I can tell some daily tasks of software developers. We develop software from customer ideas we discuss to the requirement with our teams such as design team, PC team and developer team. We choose the development platforms before we start. We develop new features and fix bugs by customers or the PC team. That’s all about the task of software development daily.


2. Do you specifically build a mobile application for business?

Yes, I do. It is technology that constantly involves innovation, so I have to constantly update for new updates. I have some function is running stable but when the system like android or ios is update to new version. This function will start because new version requires new policies. So, I have to update new features every day.


3. If so, what is the most difficult or challenging part in doing your job?

The difficult of a software developer is that I have to update new features every day.


4. What kinds of people do you interact with? Are they other IT professionals? Clients? Investors? The general public?

Usually, I will work directly with the project manager, BA, PC, and the developers. I rarely talk directly to customers. I care about the technical, I work with the project manager and with other teams.


5. Where do you spend most of your time when doing your job?

I work at the company full time. In my free time I also work overtime on other projects and I do something the work may not be busy but when it comes to the day release application to the customer. I will be more busy.


Do your work schedule is flexible, specifically, do you have time for personal stuff?

I have time for doing something because as I said I will be busy when release the application to the customer otherwise I will not be busy.


7. What aspect of your position is the most interesting to you?

I like to learn new technologies, apply social problems to software to make them easier.
